Rhino Camp is a large, long-running refugee settlement in northwestern Uganda, primarily hosting South Sudanese refugees.
While many of the frameworks (land allocation, zones, services) are in place,
significant challenges remain water quality/access, sanitation, food security, environmental degradation, farming viability, protection and social cohesion.
At the same time, there are promising initiatives around education, agro-forestry, community information and legal identity rights .
