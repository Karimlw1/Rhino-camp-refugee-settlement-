Rhino Camp Facilities

Explore our key locations in Rhino Camp. Each area is designed to provide essential services and a safe space for refugees. Hover over a card to see its location on the map.

Main Settlement

The central area where families live. Structured shelters, communal spaces, and sanitation facilities ensure safety and comfort.

Health Center

Medical facility providing general healthcare, vaccinations, maternal care, and emergency services for the community.

Education Hub

Offers formal education, literacy programs, and vocational training for children and youth within the camp.
